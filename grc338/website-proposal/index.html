Introduction

ARK

A clothing retailer that features multiple brands/deisgners. Everything head to toe that involves style is featured here. You can shop any look from various sellers depending on your desired look.

Target audience

The target audience here would be anyone looking to change or develop their wardrobe. Alongside those who are simply looking for inspiration.

The primary goal for users who visit ARK.com is to provide an easy to use platform for them to see new styles and develop their own. Complexity is often the enemy of execution, and if users want to gain anything from their experience, it has to be easy to use and easy to understand.
